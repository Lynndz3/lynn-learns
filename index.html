About Me

Hey there. My name is Lynn. In Sept 2022, I decided to switch careers from Product Management to Software Development. Why? 1. a slightly obsessive interest in the technical implementation of every feature, 2. enjoyment from working with software developers. 3. a nagging curiousity.

While I can't say exactly where this journey will take me, I'm excited to dive in. I'm excited to learn. And I'm excited to share my experiences coming from the business side.
